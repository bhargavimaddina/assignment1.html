nature overview

humans are part of nature, human activity is often understood as a separate category from other natural phenomena

advantages of nature

  • improve your mood.
  • reduce feelings of stress or anger.
  • improve your physical health.
  • connect you to your local community.

disadvantages of nature

  1. Littering is a problem.
  2. Destruction of natural habitats.
  3. You may lose some of your amenities.
  4. You may have to drive long distances.
